About Us

At MinuteMart, we believe that time is the most valuable commodity. That’s why our mission is simple — to deliver groceries and daily essentials to your doorstep in under 10 minutes. Founded with the vision of making everyday shopping faster, smarter, and more convenient, MinuteMart is here to redefine the way urban India shops.

We operate with a network of strategically located micro-warehouses that stock the most in-demand products. Using advanced logistics, real-time inventory tracking, and hyperlocal delivery models, we ensure that your order is processed and delivered in record time.

Our technology-driven platform personalizes your shopping experience, suggests frequently purchased items, and ensures seamless order placement and tracking. Whether you need fresh fruits, snacks, cleaning supplies, or personal care — we've got you covered.

MinuteMart is built by a passionate team of problem-solvers, engineers, and customer champions. We are constantly innovating to improve speed, selection, and service. Our commitment to sustainability also means we’re working toward greener deliveries and eco-friendly packaging.

Thank you for trusting MinuteMart. We're not just another delivery app — we’re your everyday shopping partner.
